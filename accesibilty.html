Statement for 4s Fashion
At 4s Fashion, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We strive to provide a positive, inclusive online shopping experience that allows all visitors to easily browse, shop, and engage with our content.
Commitment
We aim to meet or exceed the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.
We continuously review and update our website to improve accessibility and usability.
We design our content with clear navigation, readable text, and compatibility across devices and assistive technologies.
Features
Text alternatives for images and non-text content.
Keyboard-friendly navigation.
Consistent headings and structure for clarity.
Adjustable font sizes and responsive layouts.
Ongoing Efforts Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly test our website and welcome feedback to identify and address any barriers.
